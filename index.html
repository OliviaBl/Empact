In partnership with weforest

WeForest's mission is to make the earth cooler by reforesting the planet. WeForest engages with corporates, organisations and scientists to collectively advance forest landscape restoration. Since its creation in 2008, WeForest has planted over 14 million trees and reforested close to 12,000 hectares.

For its first project, E-mpact is working together with Weforest on a reforestation project in Northern Ethiopia, Tigray's region. The goal is to plant 100.000 trees and restore dry Afromontane forests to combat adverse climate change effects.
